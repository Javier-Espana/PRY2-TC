# Gramática de ejemplo para frases sencillas en inglés
Variables: S, NP, VP, V, Det, N, PP, P, Pronoun
Terminals: She, eats, a, the, cake, fork, with
Start: S
Rules:
  S -> NP VP
  VP -> V NP | VP PP
  NP -> Det N | Pronoun | NP PP
  PP -> P NP
  V -> eats
  Det -> a | the
  N -> cake | fork
  P -> with
  Pronoun -> She
